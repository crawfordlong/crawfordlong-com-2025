Fun fact: People wait hours to ride this elevator. If you walk up the stairs, past the elevator, turn left on the next street, go about 100 feet, turn into a cut-through, then walk up a couple of flights of stairs to the right to the Carmo Convent ruins. Facing the convent, turn right and ... it's the top of the lift. You can enjoy the view or pay a small additional fee to reach the very top, all without waiting in line for an elevator for hours.
